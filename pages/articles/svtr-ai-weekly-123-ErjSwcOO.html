SVTR.AI周报 是由硅谷科技评论（SVTR.AI）出品的一份人工智能行业观察周刊，依托AI创投库和专业社区资源，聚焦全球AI领域的投融资动态、技术突破和创业趋势。内容由社区成员共同策划，每周精选重点融资案例、创新企业、前沿研究成果以及行业战略分析，致力于以一线视角为AI创业者和投资人提供真实、有价值、具前瞻性的行业洞察，帮助读者及时把握行业风向和潜在机遇。

SVTR.AI Weekly is a publication by Silicon Valley Technology Review (SVTR), grounded in a robust AI investment database and an active startup community. It focuses on global developments in AI funding, cutting-edge technologies, and entrepreneurial trends. Curated collaboratively by community members, each issue highlights key funding events, innovative companies, breakthrough research, and strategic insights. With a front-line perspective, the newsletter aims to deliver timely, practical, and forward-looking intelligence to AI founders and investors—helping readers quickly grasp industry trends and seize emerging opportunities.
